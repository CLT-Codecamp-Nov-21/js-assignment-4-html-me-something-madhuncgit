My First Overseas Trip. A tri-country trip in the late 1990s.

  • Singapore
  • Malaysia
  • Sri Lanka

Singapore

My first plane trip was to Singapore in 1998.I remember being both excited and anxious,as internet was not very robust then and the flight safety data was not very well documented.It soon proved to be one of the most memorable trips of my life.Two interesting titbits about Singapore - chewing gum is banned and all 18 year old male Singaporean citizens are mandated to serve two years in the uniformed services

Malaysia

Setting aside the fact that we were mugged by our cab driver in Kuala Lumpur,I remember being very awe struck by the Petronas Towers,the tallest building in South East Asia in 1990s.

Sri Lanka

A land rich in bio-diversity,Sri Lanka holds high cultural significance among Hindus and Buddhists.It is considered as the holy land of Rama by the Hindus and a tooth from Buddha sits under the roof of a temple in a place called Kandy,thus venerated by Buddhists.
